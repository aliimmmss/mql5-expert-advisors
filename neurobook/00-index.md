## About This Book

In the era of digital technology and artificial intelligence, algorithmic trading is transforming financial markets, offering innovative strategies. This book serves as a unique guide that combines advanced technological knowledge with practical guidance on creating trading algorithms for the MetaTrader 5 platform.

The book has 7 chapters that cover everything you need to know to get started with neural networks and integrate them into your trading robots in MQL5. Beginning with basic principles of neural networks and advancing to more complex architectural solutions and attention mechanisms.

### Key Topics Covered:

- **Chapter 1** - Introduction to AI and neural networks in trading
- **Chapter 2** - Neural network fundamentals: neurons, activation functions, weight initialization, learning algorithms
- **Chapter 3** - MetaTrader 5 platform capabilities for algorithmic trading
- **Chapter 4** - Building your first neural network model in MQL5
- **Chapter 5** - Convolutional and recurrent neural network layers
- **Chapter 6** - Attention mechanisms (Self-Attention, Multi-Head Attention)
- **Chapter 7** - Batch Normalization, Dropout, and other convergence improvements
- **Chapter 8** - Testing trading capabilities with the neural network model
- **Chapter 9** - Conclusion
